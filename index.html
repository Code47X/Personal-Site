My Projects

Gottman Relationship Survey

Our team of 5 Dev Bootcamp students were asked by The Gottman Institute to take their 200 page, proprietary relationship survey and transform it into a sleek and efficient web application. This application takes the user through the survey one section at a time, and sends the calculated results to their clinician upon completion. An administrator control pannel was also designed, which allows an admin to accept new clinicians to the network and freeze current accounts if necessary. This web app will be used by hundreds of clinicians in the Gottman's network to help diagnose and treat patients relationships. Security and privacy were big areas of concentration due to the nature of the survey. Techologies used included Ruby on Rails, RSpec, BCrypt, Bootstrap, and many others.

Due to the proprietary information available in the code the repo is private. Sections of the code may be shown upon request.
